Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Overview

Pterocos is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account to use the Service. Your code and projects are stored locally in your browser using localStorage.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate the Service:

  • Usage data: Anonymous aggregate data about which features are used, collected through standard web analytics.
  • IP address: Your IP address may be logged temporarily for security purposes (e.g., rate-limiting abuse of the share feature). IP addresses are hashed and not stored in readable form.
  • Shared project content: If you use the project sharing feature, the content you share is stored on our servers to generate your share link. You may include an optional display name.

3. Local Storage

Your projects, settings, and preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the share feature. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the Service

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect the functionality of the Service.
